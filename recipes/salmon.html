Lemon Butter Salmon

Salmon is one of the best foods for you. This salmon recipe is the best one you'll find around town. With delicious additives, this dish will be a favorite.

Ingredients

  • Wild or Farmed Salmon: you'll need 1 pound of salmon in this recipe. You can choose wild or farmed
  • Butter: using a fat in the lemon honey garlic marinade is key
  • Sweetener: in this recipe I use honey, but you can use maple syrup if you prefer
  • Zing: we're adding "zing" from fresh lemon juice, lemon zest, and grainy dijon mustard
  • Seasonins: you'll also mix garlic such. Cloves or garlic powder will do

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400 degree F.
  2. Generously season your salmon with amazing lemon garlic honey marinate.
  3. Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper or foil and grease lightly with olive oil or nonstick cooking spray.
  4. Place salmon skin side down on a baking sheet. Pour/brush marinade over the top of the salmon.
  5. For every inch of thickness, bake for 15 minutes.
