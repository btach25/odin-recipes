Pizza

This pizza recipe is so delicious that everyone will love. This is a family recipe that has been in ours for many generations. If you want to add to the family tradition of making pizza we are confident this pizza will be enjoyed by everyone!

Ingredients

  • 1 14.5-ounce can whole peeled tomatoes undraines
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried aregano
  • Kosher salt
  • 1 1-pound package refrerated pizza dough
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella, sliced, or 4 shredded provolone
  • 8 fresh basil leaves, torn

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 450 degrees F. In a small saucepan, over medium heat, combine the tomatoes and their juices, the oregano, and 1/4 teaspoon salt. Bring to a boil.
  2. Reduce heat and simmer, crushing the tomatoes with a wooden sppon until the sauce thickens, 10 to 15 minutes.
  3. Shape the dough into one 12- to 14-inch round and place on a baking sheet. Cover with the sauce and top with the cheese.
  4. Bake until the crus is golden brown and crisp, about 20 minutes.
  5. Transfer to a cutting board and sprinkle with the basil. Slice and divide among the individual plates.
